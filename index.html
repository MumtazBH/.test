Get a message on the screen when you close your kindle:

Add "?alert('Message content here')" after the current URL. Make sure to close your kindle before dismissing the alert!

Calculator:

Add "?25*4+1" and replace "25*4+1" with whatever calculator expressions you want to use.

Clock:

Add "?Date()" and a clock will appear!
